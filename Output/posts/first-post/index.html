Writing with My Own Voice in the Age of AI

In today's rapidly evolving world of artificial intelligence, information is everywhere. Smart assistants can generate content quickly, sometimes replacing parts of the writing process. But I still insist on writing with my own thoughts and experiences.

Not because I don't recognize AI's power, but because I believe: Only by using my own voice can I truly reach the heart and connect with others.

This blog is a record of my continuous journey of discovery, and a space where I thoughtfully share more of my unique stories and heartfelt insights.

I welcome you to join me on this path.

— Kane
